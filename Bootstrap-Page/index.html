Cougar Nation

BYU Athletics has a proud tradition of excellence across all sports. From LaVell Edwards Stadium to the Marriott Center, Cougar fans show up with unmatched energy and passion. This site is dedicated to celebrating BYU athletics, the student athletes who represent the school, and the culture that makes being a Cougar fan one of the best experiences in all of college sports. Rise and Shout!

BYU Football

BYU Football is the crown jewel of Cougar athletics. With a storied history that includes a national championship in 1984 under the legendary LaVell Edwards, the Cougars have produced Heisman Trophy winners, NFL stars, and unforgettable moments. Now competing in the Big 12, BYU football is on the rise and playing on the biggest stage in college football.

BYU Basketball

The Marriott Center is one of the loudest arenas in college basketball when the Cougars are rolling. BYU Basketball has a tradition of producing elite guards and reaching the NCAA Tournament. With passionate home fans and a program that consistently competes at a high level, Cougar basketball is must-watch entertainment from November through March.

NIL and BYU Athletics

Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) has transformed college athletics, and BYU is no exception. Cougar athletes now have the opportunity to build their personal brands and earn compensation while competing at the highest level. NIL has changed recruiting, team dynamics, and the overall landscape of college sports in ways that are still unfolding across the country.
